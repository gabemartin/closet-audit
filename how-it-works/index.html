How Closet Audit works

Think of it as the app version of all those "rent my closet" Instagram accounts — but way better.

01

Browse or create a closet

Find closets in your town or campus. See what's available, check prices, and read rental rules — all in one place instead of scrolling an IG grid.

02

Rent at listed prices

See the price upfront. No more DMing "how much?" and waiting. Request to rent, pick your dates, and Venmo the owner. Done.

03

Wear it, return it

Rock the look for your formal, gameday, or date party. Return it within the rental window. Both sides rate the experience.

04

Earn from your own closet

List pieces you're willing to rent out. Set your own prices and rules. Make money from clothes that are just sitting in your closet.
